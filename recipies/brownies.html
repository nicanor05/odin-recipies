Brownies

Description

Fudgy and chocolaty-tasting brownie. This brownies are amazeing, in only 30 minutes you will have soft and delicious brownie!

Ingredients

  • ½ cup white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1½ cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 2 eggs
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ⅔ cup all-purpose flour
  • ¼ teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
